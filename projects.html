Projects

Welcome to my project repository!

Most of these projects are various things that have piqued my interest in the recent past, and what you see here are the results of my exploration. In addition to various projects for me, you will find some that I built specifically to entertain and teach my children.

Canvas Particles

Check out this canvas project with floating particles and connecting lines. For those on desktop, the particles interact with the mouse.

Coin Flip

Need an impartial judge to determine your fate? Look no further! Check out this simple coin flip app.

Memory

Test your memorization abilities with a quick game of Memory. The objective of the game is to match image pairs which removes them from the board. Match them all, and you win!

Minesweeper

Take a trip down memory lane and enjoy the classic game called Minesweeper. The objective of the game is to clear the board without landing on a mine. Clear them all, and you win!

Sudoku

This project was initially crafted to solve Sudoku puzzles, but once you work out how to solve them, it is a short jump to creating them! Each puzzle has one valid solution and is autogenerated. Enjoy!

Typing Practice

This is typing practice inspired by Cora and her never ending motivation to practice her typing. We'll have her typing faster than her momma in no time.

Reading Practice

Lil' miss is learning to read! She's gaming the system and remembering word orders so she's graduated to random ordering.
