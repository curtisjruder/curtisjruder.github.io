Projects

Welcome to my project repository!

Most of these projects are various things that have peaked my interest in the recent past, and what you see here are the results of my exploration. In addition to various projects for me, you will find some that I built specifically to entertain and teach my children.

Memory

Test your memorization abilities with a quick game of Memory. The objective of the game is to match image pairs which removes them from the board. Match them all, and you win!

Minesweeper

Take a trip down memory lane and enjoy the classic game called Minesweeper. The objective of the game is to clear the board without landing on a mine. Clear them all, and you win!

Canvas Particles

Check out this canvas project with floating particles and connecting lines. For those on desktop, the particles interact with the mouse.
